PodPursuit: Scaling Kubernetes Cluster

We stress-tested CoreDNS in Kubernetes, evaluating Vertical Pod Autoscaler's (VPA) resource management under high loads. Using Prometheus and Grafana for monitoring, we found that while CoreDNS handles significant traffic, stress increases resource spikes. The VPA effectively ensured stability, emphasizing the value of autoscaling in cloud-native reliability.

We developed a railway obstacle detection system using ultrasonic and IR sensors to enhance train safety.
